Classic Pizza Recipe (with Dough)

Description

Have you been searching for the perfect homemade pizza dough recipe ?
it's so easy, whips up really quick and you can make pizza whenever you want

Really easy and to point, enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups warm water (180ml).
  • 1 total tbsp granulated sugar used.
  • 1 packet yeast (7 grams) any yeast will do
  • 2 cups All Purpose Flour (240 grams) / bread flour
  • 3/4 salt
  • Oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Temperature:
     Make sure the yeast is in a warm environment,
    so fill the glass pot with hot water first and let it get warm then drop the water. now use warm water about 100 degrees, not cold or hot.

  2. Pre-mix:
     Add about 3/4 cup of warm water, half tbsp of sugar, packet of yeast,
    now stir the mix and cover it up for about 5-7 minutes till some bubbles come up on surface.

  3. Dry-mix:
     in another bowl, put the 2 cups of flour, 3/4 tsp of salt,
    the remaining 1/2 tbsp of sugar and give the combination a whisk.

  4. Mixing ingredients:
     Now into steering the Ready yeast in bowl 1 with bowl 2 dry ingredients,
    using any tool but we will use the dough hook attached to a stand mixer. Now into whisking the yeast and adding our flower bit by bit,
    it will take a couple of minutes to get into good form.
    you can add a little flour if it's wet or a sparkle of water if it feels a bit dry.

  5. Preparing the dough:
     sprinkle some flour on a flat surface and make the mixed dough into a ball,
    Bring a bowl and cover it with some oil, put the dough in the bowl and cover it too.
    Leave it in a warm cozy place, an oven can be a good choice.

  6. Rolling the dough:
     Now it the dough is doubled in size, punch it down to bring air out,
    and start rolling the dough on the flat surface, can lift it and stretch it out
    and pinch the borders, brush the surface with a bit of oil (especially the crust)

  7. Final-step:
     Add tomato sauce, cheese preferably mozzarella and whatever toppings you would like. Leave it into the over 425 temp, for about 12 minutes or so.

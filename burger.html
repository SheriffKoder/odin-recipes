Homemade Burger Recipe

Description

Ever wanted to make a heavy burger at home that tastes like outdoor restaurants
with a more healthy approach and also have a lighter effect on your wallet ?
Here's my recipe

Really easy and to point, enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Minced meat (250g ram or what suits you)
  • Burger bun
  • Cooking oil
  • Salad: Lettuce, pickles, tomato
  • Ranch or Mayo or any sauce preferred
  • Cheese slice (optional)

Steps

  1. The burger patty: pre heat
     take the minced meat into your palm, make it into a ball and try to press a litle bit
    while making shape to make it more firm and keep its shape for later
    then punch it down to take the shape of a burger patty.
    preferred to leave in freezer for a couple of minutes to take shape.

  2. The burger patty: heat
     Bring a skillet, put in it a bit of water and leave the patty for some time
    to get cooked about 40-45 mins for a well-done.

  3. The burger patty: prepare
     remove any excess water from the skillet or move the patty
    to another skillet as the meat is cooked and still have moisture inside
    from the water (if patty stored, pre heat it with some water to keep the moisture inside),
    place 1.5-2 tbsp of cooking oil under the patty and let it take
    a darker color a couple of minutes each size, medium flame.
    the cheese slice can be put when the patty is finished, will melt in seconds.

  4. Making the sandwich:
     Warm up the burger bun on a skillet for a minute (can add some butter for taste).
    place on the lower level the lettuce slice, above it the cooked patty, above the patty some pickles.
    now on the upper bun slice put some sauce and the tomato slices, place that bun over the patty
